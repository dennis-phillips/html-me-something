Berlin

Cold War Battleground, Soccer Lover Mecca

Berlin was another amazing place I was fortunate enough to visit in my recent travels
what I liked best about Berlin besides it's fascinating history was it's vibrant culture.
Berlin is a melting-pot city. I especially like the former East Berlin areas. Like Mauerpark,
and Kreuzberg. I went to many soccer games in Berlin ranging from Bundesliga 1 games featuring Hertha BSC,
to Bundesliga 2 matches featuring FC Union Berlin, and Bundesliga regional team matches featuring BFC Dynamo.
Its safe to say I'm a huge soccer fan, especially German soccer!

Ohrid

Lakeside Resort in the Southern Balkans, Key City in Slavic Language History

Ohrid is a small town located on Lake Ohrid in Macedonia. Lake Ohrid spans over parts of Macedonia and Albania.
Ohrid was an amazing place to visit, still very rustic and off the main tourist paths. The people were very friendly
and hospitable. I enjoyed touring the small byzantine style orthodox churches in Ohrid. I learned while I was there, that
the cyrillic aphabet was codified in Ohrid and the first missionaries to the Slavs actually departed from Ohrid. Old Church
Slavonic, the mother alphabet of all modern slavic languages, originated in this small town.
